Gender distribution among biographies

We started with a quick fact-check. Is it true that the gender presence is so unbalanced? Actually, it is. According to our research, only 16.5% of the biographies correspond to women, while 83.4% are related to a man. The percentage missing corresponds to biographies of people who does not identify with any of the two classical gender assignments.

One thing you may think at this point is, "okey but not everyone appears in Wikipedia". This is actually true. Although it is possible to create your own Wikiepdia page, most biographies belong to people recognized in their field. So one detail we deicded to explore further was the occupation of the different personalities.

We saw there was a huge variety of occupations, ranging from very general descriptions like “writer” or “athlete” to more detailed ones such as “midfielder” for footballers or “songwriter”. In order to obtain some informative results, we grouped the different occupations into 10 fields.
