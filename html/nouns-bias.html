We evaluated the subjectivity of the obtained adjectives using the subjectivity lexicon dictionary. Results show that adjectives related to men are weakly subjective, and most of them have a negative connotation. In contrast, the ones related to women are mostly strong subjective and have a positive connotation. Therefore, the overviews portraying females are more likely to contain subjectivity.
